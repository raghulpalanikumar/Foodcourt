## Why These Changes?

### **1. 100% Authentic**
- **Critical for mobile accessories** - Customers worry about fake products
- **Builds trust** - Assures quality and originality
- **Competitive advantage** - Differentiates from sellers of counterfeit goods

### **2. Fast Delivery**
- **Customer expectation** - Mobile accessories are often needed urgently
- **Realistic timeframe** - 2-5 days is achievable across India
- **Better than generic shipping** - More specific and actionable

### **3. Warranty Support**
- **Essential for electronics** - Customers expect warranty on chargers, earphones, etc.
- **6-12 months** - Standard warranty period for accessories
- **Adds value** - Shows commitment to product quality

### **4. Easy Returns (7 days)**
- **Realistic policy** - 7 days is standard for electronics
- **Specific condition** - "damaged items" sets clear expectations
- **Protects business** - Prevents abuse while maintaining customer satisfaction
